East Side LEAF & YARD WASTE COLLECTION update

Curbside fall leaf and yard waste collection is underway.

Our East Side collection crews are currently collecting leaves in the Tuesday refuse collection district. We anticipate being in the Wednesday and Thursday areas the week of 11/30 and the Friday and Monday areas the week of 12/7.

This estimated is based upon the best available information we have at this time. It is subject to change due to workload, weather conditions and equipment availability.

There are no set number of rounds for fall leaf collection we will continue to collect material as long as weather permits. Usually into the first week of December. Please check back to this web site for future updates and collection day estimates.

Remember: DO NOT PILE LEAVES AND YARD WASTE DIRECTLY IN THE STREET.

Leaves and yardwaste can also be brought to the drop off sites.

 

See where the crews are in your area:

  • Monday Refuse Collection area
  • Tuesday Refuse Collection area
  • Wednesday Refuse Collection area
  • Thursday Refuse Collection area
  • Friday Refuse Collection area

TIP: If you would like to minimize the amount of time your yard waste and leaf pile is at the curb, check the map for the refuse collection area a day ahead of your own. When that map is colored blue, it means crews are currently collecting from that area, and therefore it is advisable to get your material curbside at that point. This method ensures your material is ready prior to crews arriving in your neighborhood, and will shorten the amount of time the pile is on the terrace.


East Side Residents can also bring their material to the yard waste drop-off sites.



Maps are updated daily during collection periods. This page was updated on 12/2/15 at 11:43 p.m.
